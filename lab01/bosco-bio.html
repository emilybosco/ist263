Emily Bosco is a Television, Radio and Film major interested in developing and adapting books into films and TV series. Emily is skilled in script coverage, video editing and social media content creation. Emily has made Dean’s List all semesters, is a member of Syracuse University’s honors program, and was a video finalist for the Best General Entertainment Program category at the 2025 National Student Production Awards for her work on Orange Television Network's Two Truths and a Lie. Outside of work, Emily enjoys hiking with her dog, Cooper, baking, watching TV shows and films, reading, and spending time with her parents and younger sister.
